# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to building an open, respectful, and professional community around EffectOS.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation
- Assume good intent, ask for clarification before escalating
- Share context when reporting issues or proposing changes

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or abusive language
- Personal attacks or discriminatory behavior
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration spaces
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject content that violates this code.
Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.
